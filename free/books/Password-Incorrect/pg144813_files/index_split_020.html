Introduction

Password Incorrect is a selection of my 25 best tech-absurd, mobile fiction stories. Sometimes funny and sometimes mean, they’re addressing the world of the contemporary mobile reader… and spot the absurd of our present day lives: fights with the less and less comprehensible equipment, pursuit of the latest technological news, pitfalls of our modern lifestyle, useless inventions and issues racing in all directions at a breakneck speed. A lot of entertainment and a little food for thought.

Court Merrigan from TeleRead wrote: “You not likely come across anything quite like Password Incorrect any time soon. Unless this work receives the wide audience it deserves and imitators spring up.”
